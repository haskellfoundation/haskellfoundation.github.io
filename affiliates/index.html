--- title: Affiliates ---
We invite existing Haskell projects, committees and communities to explicitly affiliate and align themselves with the Haskell Foundation. Affiliation means that the group supports the goals of the Haskell Foundation, and that the Haskell Foundation in turn supports this group.
We require affiliated groups to adhere to guidelines around transparency, being open to new members, and follow the Haskell committee guidelines for respectful communication. We believe that this will help us grow towards being a more open and welcoming community.
